What is Sapio?

Sapio is the anagram of Open Source API. It provides the compatibility of an Android application running on a device without Google Play Services — i.e. deGoogled bare AOSP devices, coupled or not with microG.

Evaluations in Sapio are given to the community by the community. Sapio can serve as a lobbying tool by sharing compatibility on social media to raise awareness among app developers about respecting users' personal data.
